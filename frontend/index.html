About Us

Sheltering Souls

What are we?

Welcome to Sheltering Souls, Mysore's haven for homeless pets. Since our establishment in 2020, we've dedicated ourselves to the mission of rescuing, nurturing, and finding loving homes for animals in need.

Through the dedication of our volunteers and the unwavering support of our community, we provide shelter, care, and hope to every soul that comes through our doors.

Transformation Of Souls

At Sheltering Souls, adoption is not just a transaction—it's a transformation. Since our humble beginnings, we've witnessed countless heartwarming stories of pets finding their forever families and humans finding unconditional love. Join us in our journey to make a difference, one pawprint at a time.


Together,
